# Code of Conduct 🌳

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

The Token Forest community is a garden: it grows best when everyone tends it kindly.

## Our standards

**Do:** be welcoming and patient, assume good intent, give constructive feedback, respect differing viewpoints and experience levels, and keep discussions on topic.

**Don't:** harass, insult, or demean others; publish someone's private information; spam or troll; or make the space unpleasant in ways that would make a reasonable person want to leave.

This applies in all project spaces — issues, discussions, and any official community channels — and when representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to **[contact@tokenforest.com.au](mailto:contact@tokenforest.com.au)**. Reports are reviewed by the maintainers, handled confidentially, and may result in warnings, comment removal, or bans at the maintainers' discretion.

This code of conduct is adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), v2.1.
